# Miraculous Bot v7

## New in v7

### /panel (Admin Only)
Only members with a **higher role position than the bot** (or server owner / Administrator) can use this.
Opens a panel with 4 buttons:
- 🔄 **Reset ALL Miraculouses** — Wipes every holder's Miraculous
- 🎁 **Give Miraculous** — Browse and assign Miraculouses
- 🪙 **Give Charms** — Grant Charms to users
- 💸 **Wipe Economy** — Reset all Charms balances

### Chrysalis (not Shadow Monarch!)
The Butterfly Miraculous villain form is now correctly named **Chrysalis**, with matching dialogue.

### Ability Damage System
Powers now have a **miss chance** and some have a **kill chance**:
- **Miss** → flavored miss message, no effect
- **Kill** → target's Miraculous is wiped, they get a DM saying who killed them
- Cataclysm: 50% kill, 20% miss
- Collision (Tiger): 40% kill, 15% miss
- Resistance (Ox): 30% kill, 10% miss
- Weather (Dragon): 35% kill, 20% miss
- Uproar (Monkey): 20% kill, 30% miss

### Economy System 🪙
- When you get a Miraculous, choose **Greater Good** (+100 Charms) or **Evil** (no reward)
- `/patrol` — go on patrol every 5 minutes to earn 100–500 Charms randomly
- `/balance` — check your Charms balance
- `/leaderboard` — top 10 Charms holders
